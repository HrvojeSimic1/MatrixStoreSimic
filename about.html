About Us

We are a small webshop from Slavonski Brod, a town in the east of Croatia selling musical equipment. Started off by a student in Tehnicka Škola Slavonski Brod for a project that he will most likely get a B on. Here is an unrelated picture of a girl playing piano.

Best music shop around

Musicians in Croatia don't get nearly as much support as people like sportsmen, especially football and basketball enjoyers, with no shops and equipment around. Want to order a new amp? Pay massive shipping. We are here to change that by opening up the first webshop based in Croatia. So tune up your instruments, a revolution in the Croatian music scene is on the way!
